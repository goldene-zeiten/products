============
Users Manual
============

On the cart page, above the usual :guilabel:`Proceed to checkout` button, shoppers using Safari on an Apple
device see an Apple Pay button.

Tapping it opens the Apple Pay sheet. The shopper picks a shipping address there, and the sheet shows the
shop's live shipping options and a running total. On authorizing with Face ID / Touch ID, the payment is
taken and the shopper lands directly on the shop's order confirmation (thank-you) page — the same page a
normal checkout ends on. No address form, shipping step or payment step is shown.

On non-Apple browsers the button does not appear, and the shopper continues through the normal checkout
unaffected.
