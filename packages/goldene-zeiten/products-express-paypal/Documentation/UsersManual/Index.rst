============
Users Manual
============

On the cart page, above the usual :guilabel:`Proceed to checkout` button, the shopper sees a PayPal button.

Tapping it opens PayPal's own window. The shopper logs in and picks a shipping address there, and the sheet
total updates to include the shop's shipping for that address. On approving, the payment is taken and the
shopper lands directly on the shop's order confirmation (thank-you) page — the same page a normal checkout
ends on, with the same order, invoice and withdrawal links. No address form, shipping step or payment step
is shown.

If PayPal is not available, or the basket cannot be shipped to the chosen address, the button either does
not appear or PayPal reports the address as unserviceable, and the shopper continues through the normal
checkout unaffected.
