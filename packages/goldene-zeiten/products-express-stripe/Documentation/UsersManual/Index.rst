============
Users Manual
============

On the cart page, above the usual :guilabel:`Proceed to checkout` button, the shopper sees an express
wallet button — Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, Amazon Pay or Link, depending on their browser and device.

Tapping it opens the wallet's own sheet. The shopper picks a saved shipping address there, and the sheet
shows the shop's live shipping options and a running total. On confirming, the payment is taken and the
shopper lands directly on the shop's order confirmation (thank-you) page — the same page a normal checkout
ends on, with the same order, invoice and withdrawal links. No address form, shipping step or payment step
is shown.

If no wallet is available on the shopper's device, or the basket cannot be shipped to the chosen address,
the button simply does not appear (or the wallet reports the address as unserviceable), and the shopper
continues through the normal checkout unaffected.
