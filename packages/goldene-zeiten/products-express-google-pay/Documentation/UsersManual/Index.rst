============
Users Manual
============

On the cart page, above the usual :guilabel:`Proceed to checkout` button, shoppers with Google Pay available
see a Google Pay button.

Tapping it opens the Google Pay sheet. The shopper picks a shipping address there, and the sheet shows the
shop's live shipping options and a running total. On authorizing, the payment is taken and the shopper lands
directly on the shop's order confirmation (thank-you) page — the same page a normal checkout ends on. No
address form, shipping step or payment step is shown.

Where Google Pay is not available, the button does not appear and the shopper continues through the normal
checkout unaffected.
